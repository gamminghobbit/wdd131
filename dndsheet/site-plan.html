Site Name:

Hardy's 3.5 digital Character sheet

Site Purpose:

This website will help users make and store character sheets for Dungeons & Dragon's 3.5th edition.

Scenarios:

  • What are a few questions users might want answered on your site?
  • How can I create a digital character sheet for D&D 3.5e?
  • Can I share the characters I make with my Dungeon Master, and fellow Players?
  • Will this site be something I can use with a mobile device like a phone or tablet?

Color Scheme:

Main: #444 | Accent: #D4AF37

Typography:

Header Font: Aclonica
Body Font: 'Alike', serif
